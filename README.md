# Smitherson Capital – Business & Digital Transformation Capstone

This repository contains a complete **Business Analysis Capstone Project** focused on the **Business and Digital Transformation** of Smitherson Capital Investment Firm, a legacy financial advisory organization facing modernization challenges.

The project follows the **end-to-end Business Analysis lifecycle**, from problem definition and stakeholder analysis to scope control, change management, final delivery, and project closure.

---

## Project Background

Smitherson Capital Investment Firm is a 138-year-old financial advisory firm with:
- An aging client base (average age 73)
- An aging advisor workforce
- Low digital adoption
- Increasing competition from digitally advanced financial institutions

To remain relevant and competitive, leadership initiated a **Business & Digital Transformation Project** aimed at:
- Retaining generational wealth
- Attracting younger, tech-savvy investors
- Modernizing digital services
- Expanding into new Canadian markets

---

## Project Objectives

- Modernize client engagement through digital platforms
- Transition from paper-based to digital processes
- Recruit and onboard a diverse advisory workforce
- Improve stakeholder engagement and governance
- Enable scalable national expansion
- Ensure controlled scope and change management

---

## Repository Structure

Project lifecycle:

**01 – Case Study**  
  Project background, business problem, and organizational context  

**02 – Business Analysis Report**  
  Organizational, project, and client analysis (KYO, KYP, KYC)

**03 – Project Scope & Requirements Traceability Matrix (RTM)**  
  Defined in-scope/out-of-scope boundaries and traceability of business needs

**04 – Stakeholder Analysis & KPIs**  
  Stakeholder roles, analytics needs, success metrics, and product roadmap

**05 – Rules of Engagement & RACI Matrix**  
  Governance model, team responsibilities, and accountability framework

**06 – Change Request Management**  
  Formal change request for expansion to Vancouver and Ottawa, including impact analysis

**07 – Business Memo (Executive Communication)**  
  Approval request for presentation topic, audience, and learning objectives

**08 – Final Project Presentation**  
  Executive-level summary of analysis, recommendations, roadmap, risks, and outcomes

**09 – Project Closure & Lessons Learned**  
  Final deliverables acceptance, financial closure, lessons learned, and recommendations
